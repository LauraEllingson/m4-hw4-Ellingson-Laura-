About

Post Malone is an American rapper, singer and songwriter. He was born Austin Richard Post on July 4th, 1995 in Syracuse, New York. At the age of nine, he and his family moved to Grapevine, TX.

In 2015, Post Malone released, "White Iverson," which secured him a spot in the top 20 Billborad 100, which earned him a record deal with Republic Records. Post Malone currently lives in Los Angeles California.
